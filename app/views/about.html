About me.

My name is Kellie Petersen. I'm a self taught developer from Australia. I switched careers from reading law to writing code because of my passion for coding. I also attempt designing things.

Who I am

I'm Kellie, a front-end developer who sometimes attempts designing things. I like turning interesting ideas into usable products.

I value learning new tools, writing readable and maintainable code, and creating responsive and accessible websites.

Where I started

I grew up in a tiny town nobody's ever heard of, in QLD Australia. I began coding during college as a hobby, and it developed into a passion.

I moved to the USA 2 years ago, and I'm currently living near Atlanta, Georgia.

What I do

I'm currently working for Novelle Web & Software, a small business I co-founded with my husband.

In my spare time, I work on my own small projects, try out the infinite list of front-end tools, and contribute to open source.
